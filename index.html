RLetters

Welcome to the website for RLetters, an open-source Rails web application designed for the maintenance of a large database of academic journal articles. Article content is stored in a Solr backend, and tools are provided to perform various text-mining and digital humanities work on the journal text.

RLetters is developed by the Digital Humanities Working Group at the University of Notre Dame. The lead developer is Charles Pence.

RLetters has not yet been released, but is in active closed beta development. You can check out a live deployment of RLetters by visiting the evoText project.
